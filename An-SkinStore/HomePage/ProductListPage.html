Skin Medica

SkinMedica are a professional skincare brand, who for over 10 years have been dedicated to developing innovative skincare products. The brand lives by the philosophy that everyone deserves to have naturally radiant skin, so they look and feel confident everyday.

With this in mind, SkinMedica pride themselves with undertaking extensive research around formulating advanced skincare, so that all their products rejuvenate and care for our skin effectively.

What is SkinMedica’s bestselling products?

The TNS Recovery Complex is a leader when it comes to addressing multiple skin care concerns. For many people thesedays time is short, this could be just what the doctor ordered! As with all of the products in the TNS line, the Recovery Complex contains NouriCel- MD.

It is a research based cosmetic ingredient discovered through wound healing, composed of natural human growth factors, soluble collagen, matrix proteins and antioxidants. It is the natural human growth factors included in that potent blend which play a very important role in mimicking the skin’s natural renewal process.

As if the TNS Recovery Complex doesn’t have enough to offer, SkinMedica has also introduced another breakthrough product; The TNS Essential Serum. It’s not only theNouriCel- MD but also includes a second chamber consisting of APS Corrective Complex. This second chamber, APS Corrective Complex, contains a combination of potent antioxidants including vitamin e, vitamin c, and green tea; as well as peptides and other essential anti-aging ingredients. The APS Complex will help lighten/brighten age spots, protect the skin from environmental damage, tighten and smooth skin and also strengthen the skin’s natural ability to regenerate itself.
